- Licorice Candy -




By the early 20th century, licorice candy was a popular treat available in a variety of shapes such as pipes, golf clubs, cigarettes, among others. There are two ways to make licorice candies. Some companies use a molding process where a liquid licorice mixture is poured into individual molds. Upon cool, the candies are released from the molds and are ready to be packaged. The other method of producing licorice candy is by extrusion. This method produces licorice ropes and laces.
